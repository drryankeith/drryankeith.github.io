Ryan J. Keith

I am an impactful educator and social-ecological scientist with eight years' experience teaching at universities, schools, and zoos. I am particularly fascinated by interactions between people and natural environments, with my research currently focused on understanding how urban children connect with nature.

Despite what this website's URL would have you believe, I'm not quite a doctor just yet. I am currently finishing up my PhD in the Integrative Ecology Lab at The University of Sydney, under the watchful eye of Prof Dieter Hochuli, Prof Lisa Given, and Dr John Martin. I previously completed my Masters of Teaching at UNSW and recieved my bachelor's in ecology from my current institution.
